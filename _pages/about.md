I am currently a Master's student in the College of Cryptography and Security at [Nankai University](https://www.nankai.edu.cn/), co-advised by **Prof. Zheli Liu** and **Assoc. Prof. Siyi Lv**. Prior to my master's studies, I received my Bachelor's degree in Information Security from Nankai University in 2025, graduating as an Outstanding Graduate.

My research interests center on the intersection of data security and artificial intelligence. I have practical experience ranging from open-source database kernel development to vulnerability analysis and RAG defense frameworks.

Feel free to reach out to me via email (**yangz@mail.nankai.edu.cn**) for discussions, collaborations, or **potential PhD opportunities**!
